FAQ

Do you offer financial assistance?

Yes. We provide free tutoring and sliding scale pricing for families who qualify. Contact our team to discuss your needs and eligibility.

How do I sign up a student?

Use the Tutoring Signup section on the Signup page or submit the form directly. We will follow up to schedule your first session.

What volunteer roles are available?

We welcome tutors, mentors, event helpers, administrative volunteers, and outreach supporters. We provide training and ongoing guidance.

Can I donate even if I am not local?

Absolutely. Donations are accepted online and support programs wherever there is community need.
